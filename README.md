Aika Nexus Hub eliminates tool sprawl by integrating essential business functions into one cohesive system. It is designed to scale from small startups to large enterprises, with a focus on Indonesian business workflows.

### Core Modules
- **Projects & Tasks**: Plan work, track progress, and assign ownership.
- **HR & Attendance**: Manage employees, roles, leave requests, and time tracking.
- **Finance**: Invoicing, payments, expenses, and financial reporting.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Real-time insights with custom reporting.
- **Security**: Enterprise-grade role-based access control.

### Extendable Add-ons
- **CRM**: Lead and pipeline management.
- **Payroll**: Indonesian tax handling and payroll processing.
- **Asset Tracking**: Hardware and resource management.
- **Performance Review**: KPI tracking and employee evaluations.
- **Career Site**: Integrated recruitment portal.
