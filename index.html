Everything you need to know about Aika

How does the free trial work? +
You get 14 days of full access to all features. No credit card required. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.
Can I change plans later? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and Indonesian payment methods. Enterprise plans can be invoiced.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, regular backups, and comply with international security standards.
Do you offer training and support? +
Yes! We provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and dedicated support based on your plan.
Where is my data hosted? +
Our servers are located in Indonesia, fully compliant with local data privacy regulations (PDP Law).
Do you support integrations? +
Yes, Business and Enterprise plans include API access. Pre-built integrations with major accounting software are coming soon.
Is there a mobile app? +
Yes! The Aika Nexus mobile app is available for iOS and Android, keeping your team connected anywhere.
Can I customize the platform with my branding? +
Enterprise plans allow for white-labeling, including custom domains, logos, and color schemes.
What kind of support do I get? +
All plans include community support. Paid plans get priority email and WhatsApp support with faster response times.
